# Student Panel Flow Guide

## Getting Started
1. **Sign Up / Login:**
   - Go to the login page (`/login`).
   - If you don't have an account, click "Sign up".
   - Use your email and password to access the Dashboard.
   *Tip: Use a simple password you can remember easily for testing!*

2. **The Dashboard:**
   - This is your main hub. You will see your stats (Karma Points, Day Streak) here.
   - Click "Resume Learning" to jump back into your last course.
   - Check the "Daily Challenge" card to earn extra points.

3. **Finding Courses:**
   - Click "Browse Courses" in the sidebar or "View All Courses".
   - You can search for specific topics like "Physics" or "Math".
   - Click "Enroll Now" on any course card to add it to your library.
   *Tip: Use the search bar for quick access!*

4. **Watching Lessons:**
   - Open a course from "My Courses".
   - Click on a lesson in the list.
   - Watch the video and read the notes.
   - Once done, click "Mark Complete" to track progress.

5. **Taking Tests:**
   - Go to "Mock Tests" in the sidebar.
   - Select a test and answer the questions.
   - Submit to see your score instantly.
   *Tip: Don't rush! Read each question carefully.*

6. **Asking the AI Tutor:**
   - While in a lesson, look for the "AI Chat" or "Ask AI" button.
   - Type your question about the topic.
   - The AI will answer based on the course material.
